WHY MATOSINHOS?

Matosinhos, the coastal gem

Matosinhos, where tradition meets the sea Nestled along the stunning Portuguese coastline, Matosinhos is a captivating city that offers a blend of rich traditions and coastal beauty. With a history rooted in fishing and maritime heritage, Matosinhos exudes a unique charm that draws visitors from near and far. As you explore the streets of Matosinhos, you'll encounter a vibrant atmosphere filled with local charm. From the bustling fish markets where fishermen bring in their fresh catches, to the cozy cafes serving mouthwatering seafood dishes like the renowned grilled sardines, the city offers a true gastronomic delight.

Beyond the culinary delights, Matosinhos boasts beautiful sandy beaches that stretch along its coastline, inviting visitors to relax and enjoy the refreshing Atlantic waters. The picturesque promenade is perfect for leisurely walks, offering breathtaking views of the rolling waves and the iconic Matosinhos lighthouse.

Whether you're seeking a taste of fresh seafood, the tranquility of the beach, or an exploration of local culture, Matosinhos has something to offer every visitor. Discover the allure of this coastal gem and create unforgettable memories in Matosinhos.

Mercado de Matosinhos

Mercado de Matosinhos is a vibrant market in the heart of Matosinhos, Portugal. It offers a diverse selection of fresh local produce, including fish, fruits, and vegetables. The market is a bustling hub where visitors can immerse themselves in the local culinary scene and experience the authentic atmosphere of the region.

Address:

R. França Júnior, 4450-135 Matosinhos

What I like about it

Mercado de Matosinhos delights with its fresh and diverse food offerings.

Piscina das Marés

Piscina das Marés in Leça is a captivating saltwater swimming pool designed by architect Álvaro Siza Vieira. Its seamless integration with the ocean and stunning coastal views make it a unique and memorable destination for swimming enthusiasts and architecture lovers alike.

Address:

Av. Liberdade, 4450-716 Leça da Palmeira, Portugal

What I like about it

Piscina das Marés provides a picturesque setting for swimming, allowing you to enjoy the beauty of the coastline while taking a refreshing dip.

Praia de Matosinhos

Praia de Matosinhos is a popular sandy beach known for its inviting waters and vibrant atmosphere. It offers a range of activities, from relaxing sunbathing to exciting surf sessions, making it a favorite spot for beach lovers of all kinds. With its lively boardwalk and beachside amenities, Praia de Matosinhos provides a memorable beach experience for visitors to enjoy.

Address:

Praia em Matosinhos, Portugal

What I like about it

Praia de Matosinhos's vibrant atmosphere creates an energetic and lively beach environment that is sure to enhance your enjoyment and make your beach visit memorable.
